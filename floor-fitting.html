Pricing

What You'll Pay - No Hidden Costs

We quote the job as a whole: preparing the subfloor, the fitting itself, trims, thresholds and finishing. If we are supplying the boards, the timber is priced up front too. The number on the quote is the number on the invoice.

A new floor is only as good as the subfloor under it and the acclimatisation before it. Those are the corners that get cut to make a cheap quote work, and they are exactly the corners we do not cut.

Quoted As Fitted Subfloor prep, fitting, trims and finish, all in one price. No extras invented halfway through.
How We Work

What to Expect

1
Choose the right boardSolid or engineered, plank width, finish. We talk you through what suits the room, the subfloor and the way you live, with samples in hand.
2
Prepare the subfloorLevel, dry and solid before a single board goes down. This step decides whether the floor creaks in two years, so it gets done properly.
3
Acclimatise the timberBoards sit in the room they are being fitted in until they have settled. Skipping this is how new floors end up with gaps by Christmas.
4
Fit tight, square and levelLaid with proper expansion gaps, tight joints and clean trims. We do not leave until the details are right.
