Warmer Floors

Floor Insulation in Bristol

Bristol's older houses lose serious heat through bare suspended floors. We lift the boards, insulate underneath and put everything back tight.

Pricing

What You'll Pay - No Hidden Costs

We quote per floor after seeing it, and the price covers everything: lifting the boards without wrecking them, the insulation itself, and relaying the floor so it sits tight and flat again. No extras appear on the invoice that were not on the quote.

Lifting a hundred-year-old floor is the risky part of this job. Done carelessly it splits boards and costs you the originals. We take the time to do it properly, and that is what you are paying for.

Quoted Once, Done Once The price includes lifting, insulating and relaying. Your original boards go back down, not in a skip.
How We Work

What to Expect

1
Check what is under thereWe look at the void depth, airflow and any damp signs before recommending anything. Insulation done wrong traps moisture, so this step is not optional.
2
Lift the boards carefullyBoards come up numbered and intact. Original Victorian pine is worth protecting, and we treat it that way.
3
Insulate between the joistsBreathable insulation fitted snug between the joists with the airflow under the floor preserved, so the timber stays dry.
4
Relay and finishEvery board back in its place, fixed down tight, no new creaks. If the floor is being sanded too, this is where the two jobs meet.
