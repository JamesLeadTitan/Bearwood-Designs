Local Floor Sanding You Can Trust in Westbury-on-Trym

Westbury-on-Trym mixes an old village core with streets of solid interwar family houses, and the floors range just as widely: old cottage boards, 1930s oak, and modern engineered floors in extended kitchens.

Whatever the age of the house, the approach is the same. We assess what is actually under your feet, tell you straight what it needs, and do the preparation that makes the finish last.

Floor Problems We Fix in Westbury-on-Trym

A real mix of floor types comes up in Westbury-on-Trym. We take on:

Worn and scratched boardsSanded back through the full grit sequence, never just skimmed, then finished in hardwax oil or lacquer.
Gaps between floorboardsFilled with resin and dust or timber slivers, sanded flat so the repair disappears.
Paint, bitumen and old stainStripped back to clean timber, including the stubborn black borders left by old carpet fittings.
Split, patched or missing boardsRepaired or replaced with matching reclaimed timber before any sanding starts.
Cold, draughty floorsInsulated from below or during a refit, so the room holds its heat.
