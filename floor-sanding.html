Pricing

What You'll Pay - No Hidden Costs

Every floor is different, so we quote each one after seeing it. The number we give you covers the whole job: repairs, gap filling, the full grit sequence and every coat of finish. Nothing gets added on later and nothing gets quietly skipped to hit a price.

We are not the quote that wins on price alone. We are the quote where the floor still looks right in ten years, because the preparation that decides that was actually done.

One Price, The Whole Job The figure on your quote includes the prep, the repairs and the finish. That is the number you pay.
How We Work

What to Expect

1
Look at the floorWe come out, lift a corner if needed, and tell you straight what the boards can take and what the finish options are.
2
Repairs and gap filling firstSplit boards replaced, loose boards fixed, gaps filled with resin or timber slivers before a sander touches anything.
